Although many bold people consider piranhas to be highly dangerous, they are, for the most part , entirely harmless. Piranhas rarely feed on large animals; they eat smaller fish and aquatic plants. When confronted with humans , piranhas' first instinct is to flee rather than attack. In fact, far more piranhas are eaten by people than people are eaten by piranhas. Ultimately, as long as the fish are well-fed, they will not bite humans
